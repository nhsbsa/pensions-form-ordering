As your form is not listed, send an email to nhsbsa.pensionsmember@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

In your email, include:

  • your name
  • the form you need
  • your membership number
  • your date of birth
  • the full address and postcode you want the form to be sent to

What happens next

We will read your request, find your form and send your form to you to fill in.

Your form will have guidance on what you need and where it should be sent.
